The practices of naturopathy

2021-03-23

Here’s a bit of background knowledge about naturopathy before we get into the details.

Naturopathy combines modern treatment with traditional methods, and it includes alternative, natural therapies to modern medicine. Its practices focuses on utilizing human body’s self-healing abilities, prevention of diseases, and the optimization of health even when no disease is currently present.

Naturopathy looks for underlying symptoms, and then develop customized harmless and holistic treatment for patients. it also emphasizes on educating patients about the treatment. Some specific practices of naturopathy includes diet recommendation, exercise, herbal dietary supplements, psychotherapy, detoxification, and hemeopathy.

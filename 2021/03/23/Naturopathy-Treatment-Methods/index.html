Naturopathy Treatment Methods

2021-03-23

The purpose of today’s journal will introduce you to some naturopathy practices, their philosophy, and whether there’s scientific proof of their effectiveness.

Osteopathy

The first method is osteopathy. It’s a method that treats health problems by moving, stretching, and massaging muscles and joints. It’s based on the belief that the wellbeing of an individual depends on their bones, muscles, ligaments, and connective tissue functioning smoothly together. Some techniques include:

  • massage
  • stretching stiff joints
  • articulation – moving your joints in their normal range of motion
  • high-velocity thrusts – sharp and fast movements to the spine, which produces a sound like cracking knuckles

In UK, National Institute for Health and Care Excellence (NICE) guidelines on managing lower back pain state that manual therapy can be considered as a treatment option alongside exercise.

Research published in 2012 about the effectiveness of osteopathy (OMT) for acute low back pain states in active duty military personnel states that participants treated with osteopathy experience significantly less pain immediately after the treatment compared to conventional treatments, as well as less pain 4 weeks after the treatment. This applied to all groups of people of different ages. The above image is an example of two groups.

However, whether osteopathy works for other diseases that it’s commonly used for has yet to be proved, and osteopathy isn’t always based on scientific evidence.

Homeopathy

Homeopathy uses products that come from plants. In most cases, they are made into sugar pallets that are placed under the tongue to be consumed.

Homeopathy has 2 central beliefs:

  1. “Like cures like”：a disease can be cured by a substance that produces similar symptoms in healthy people
  2. “Law of minimum dose”: the lower the dose, the greater its effectiveness. Because of this belief, some homeopathy products are extremely diluted to an extent that there’s no active ingredient left.

However, according to comprehensive research conducted by the Australian Medical Research Council in 2015, there’s no reliable evidence that homeopathy is effective for any health condition. Moreover, some of its practices contradict fundamental scientific principles. For example, it’s very hard to prove why a product without an active ingredient would have any effect.

Chiropractic

chiropractic is a method that emphasizes manual techniques such as joint adjustment and manipulation, with a specific focus on subluxation (partial dislocation of joints or organs). Some central beliefs of chiropractic include:

  1. The body is a system in which disorder in one part of the system will disturb other parts
  2. The spine and health are closely related, and this relationship is mediated by the nervous system
  3. When disorders in the body are removed, stresses on the body’s nervous system can be alleviated and the general health of the body will be restored

According to an essay published in 2015, studies proved the impact of chiropractic for improving some supper extremity conditions such as shoulder and neck trigger points, neck pain, and sports injuries. However, for other purposes such as asthma, infant colic, further research needs to be conducted to determine the result.
